Legitimate Interest. A second ground relied upon by Poshmark for other types of processing of your Personal Information is that it is necessary for the purposes of legitimate interests pursued by Poshmark. Poshmark will only rely on such a ground where an assessment has been performed balancing the interests and rights involved and the necessity of the processing in order to provide our services, products and features to you.
A Legal Obligation. A third ground relied upon for certain types of processing is that it is necessary in order to allow Poshmark to comply with a legal obligation.
Consent. Finally, in certain situations Poshmark relies on your consent in order to process your Personal Data, such as marketing communications.
